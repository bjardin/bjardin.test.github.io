My Career

You are probably here because you want to know more about me, especially on my career.

I was 11 when I got my first gig as a salesman and demonstrator. My role is to visit 12-15 malls on the weekend to demonstrate and sell yo-yos. Me as a kid, I don’t know what to do with my salary, so I just save a little bit for myself so I can eat out, and give the rest to my mom.

During those years, I learned how to market myself and buy & sell products. I market myself so I can get some gigs on the top of my weekend work, and sell some of my toys so I can buy more toys.

Then high school came. I was distracted. Dating, online games, friends, yo-yo career, gigs and school. It is so hard to prioritize things. Being a teenager, you need more than just school allowance. So I have to do more gigs and buy & sell my toys so I have budget on my dating and life adventures.

I went to Engineering school, and dropped out when I was in my third year. That year is the biggest decision I have to make. It’s depressing and probably I'm at the lowest state of my life. It’s the time I have to decide whether to stay in the Philippines with my family, friends, girlfriend and finish my Engineering degree or accept the once in a lifetime opportunity and relocate in the USA.

Fast forward now, I think I made the right decision. My girlfriend is now my wife, and we are both in USA! I didn’t pursue my Engineering degree because I realized that I’ve been doing sales and marketing for such a long time, and I feel like this is my destiny.

I’m very talkative -- I forgot you are here because you want to know more about my career! So here it goes.

I work for Duncan Toys, a division of Flambeau Incorporated. Duncan has many toy categories and a huge chunk of my work goes to high-end yo-yos, which supports the niche market and front-end of Duncan. It’s the front-end because the niche market of yo-yoing is the community. This is where we develop our cross-generation fans, word of mouth and organic marketing. Aside from the high-end products, I manage our ecommerce business, flagship stores, email marketing and events. I have been working with Duncan Toys for about 9 years. Here is the breakdown:

  • 4 years as International Brand Ambassador.
  • 3 years in Sales and Managerial role.
  • 3 years in Marketing and Sales.
